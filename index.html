About Us

VolunTree is a social platform to connect non-profit organizations with volunteers who are interested in being a part of their social welfare efforts. We wish to personify volunteers as selfless givers that bring about a positive change. Volunteers can register for an event based on their interest. We also allow representatives of various non-profits to register and populate the list of current and future events. We enable them to provide some background of their organization and its narrative. This accounts for the authenticity of these events or volunteering opportunities.

This website also serves as an ideal forum for various corporate organizations who are looking to project their company’s image as one of taking Corporate Social Responsibility. Through our website, they could also find recognition through specialized badges against the kind of service they provide. Users could also aid monetary support by making donations to various social events of their choice.
